Telenor and Vodafone pilot first international HD-Voice test

WOW 2.0 > Global > Global News Center > Telenor and Vodafone pilot first international HD-Voice test
 
16132 Yes newsarticle Persheim, Kari Johansen
08.02.2017 01:01 (UTC+01:00)
 
Page Image
Image Caption

​Global Wholesale Project Team (missing Telenor Norway's team members: Per Anders Bjørck, Thormod Schie, Helge Sandgrind, Håkon Sigstad and Rolf Nersund)

Together with Vodafone Carrier Services, Global Wolesale has set up Telenor’s first ever Voice over LTE (VoLTE) Interworking call.  The call was received in Telenor Norway’s test lab on 27 January at Telenor Fornebu Headquarters.
Page Content

Keeping voice relevant for our customers

It is no secret that OTT's have taken away a large percentage of international voice traffic from operators in recent years.  In-app calling has often delivered better voice quality than international direct dialing.  Global Wholesale successfully completed the first ever Voice over LTE (VoLTE) Interworking test call, which provided HD-Voice between Vodafone Germany and Telenor Norway.  This test is the first step towards delivering HD-Voice calls internationally, which will provide a superior customer experience.

The power of collaboration

This pilot has been an example of true collaboration across borders and companies. Telenor Norway, Global Wholesale, Vodafone Carrier Services and Vodafone engineers from Spain, Germany and Italy worked together to set up a new SIP interconnect. "We have set up new connections between all four companies in order to get the call through. There were some challenges in the initial set up but once we ironed out the issues the call completed. The call was crystal clear and it seemed like we could have been in the same room together. We expect this type of quality here in Norway and now we are able to offer it internationally," says Harald Krohg CEO of Global Wholesale.

The future of Voice

International VoLTE calls will provide richer higher quality voice services and allow Telenor to compete with Over the Top (OTT) Players and win back our customers. Voice over LTE is just the second service to be put on the IP network and now that this is available other exciting services like Rich Communications Services (RCS) can be added. The new VoLTE service is planned to launch in Q3 this year, starting in Norway.
